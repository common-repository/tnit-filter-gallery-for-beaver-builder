Just another filterable gallery plugin for Beaver Builder Plugin. Simple but flexible and extendible.

== Description ==

TNIT Filter Gallery works as module with Beaver Builder Plugin, by using you can manage multiple filter able galleries even on single page, plus you can customize gallery post counts, title show/hide, content show hide etc with ease or just with few clicks. This is custom post based galelry which can be used to show up your portfolio or your client's website gallery in nicer way.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `tnit-filter-gallery` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

You will find 'Portfolio' menu in your WordPress admin panel.
Where you can create posts and categories to show your stuff nicely over the internet on your website.
For basic usage, you can also have a look at the [plugin homepage](http://www.topnotchinv.com/).

== Screenshots ==

1. Adding new Category to Portfolio post type, that will be shown as filter able button. 
Create categories, and add portfolio posts to that desired category to show up in the filter perfectly.
2. Once you have created your categories, now move on to create your posts, add post tilte, content, attach
thumbnail, and select your desired category. Those categories will serve as filter on front end screen.
3. Once you have created your posts and attached categories, its time for magic to begin, just go to
your page where you want to use filter able gallery, Under advanced modules in beaver builder 
modules, select and drag "Filter Gallery" to your page, and chose your settings as per your need.
